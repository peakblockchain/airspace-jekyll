About Us

Located near Portsmouth, NH - Peak Blockchain is an experienced developer of solutions for the Blockchain and Cryptocurrency industries. Our founders are active Cryptocurrency investors, traders and miners who started in this industry together in 2013. Early in our Cryptocurrency journey we noticed a substantial lack of services and platforms that were available for more mature industries. This lack of services we felt was, and continues to be, a barrier for adoption of the technology. Since our inception in 2017 Peak's mission has been clear, to build solutions that allow greater and easier access to the Cryptocurrency industry.

ROADMAP

Our flagship product (PEAK) is currently under active development and nearing a Beta release. When released our PEAK platform will provide a unique and comprehensive set of services to Cryptocurrency investors, traders and miners.
